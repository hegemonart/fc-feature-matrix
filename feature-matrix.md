---

## Weighting Methodology

Features are weighted by strategic importance for football club digital benchmarking:

| Weight | Tier | Rationale |
|--------|------|-----------|
| **5** | Revenue Critical | Directly drives commercial revenue (tickets, shop, membership, streaming) |
| **4** | Engagement Core | Primary fan engagement & retention levers (content, video, match data) |
| **3** | Brand & Identity | Brand differentiation, inclusivity, development pipeline visibility |
| **2** | UX & Utility | User experience fundamentals that support all other tiers |
| **1** | Differentiators | Unique value-adds that distinguish top clubs from the rest |

## Tier Analysis

### Tier 1 — Table Stakes (90%+)
Every club must have these. Absence is a red flag.
- **News Feed** (100%), **Hero Banner** (100%), **Footer Navigation** (100%), **Social Media** (95%), **Deep Navigation** (95%)

### Tier 2 — Expected Standard (70-90%)
Most top clubs have these. Missing them puts you behind the pack.
- **E-Commerce/Shop** (85%), **Sponsor Showcase** (85%), **Video Content** (80%), **Match Center** (75%), **Ticketing** (70%), **Search** (70%)

### Tier 3 — Competitive Edge (40-70%)
Differentiates leaders from followers. Where the top 10 separate from the bottom 10.
- **Login/Registration** (63%), **Membership Programs** (53%), **App Promotion** (50%), **Academy/Youth** (48%), **Women's Team** (45%), **Streaming/OTT** (45%)

### Tier 4 — Innovation Frontier (<40%)
Early adopters only. Opportunity space for differentiation.
- **Stadium Digital** (30%), **Foundation** (25%), **Heritage** (20%), **Newsletter** (15%), **Multi-Language** (10%), **Esports** (5%), **Transfer Tracker** (0%), **Fan Feedback** (0%)

### Notable Outliers
- **Arsenal** (€821.7m revenue → 58.3% digital): Massive underperformance — content-heavy but almost zero commercial homepage integration (no shop showcase, no membership, no streaming)
- **Tottenham** (€672.6m → 76.4%): Outperforms revenue tier — strong streaming (SpursPlay), membership (One Hotspur), stadium multi-use
- **Inter Milan** (€537.5m → 70.8%): Punches well above weight with Inter TV, strong shop, match center
- **West Ham** (€276m → 36.8%): Weakest digital presence — no shop, no tickets, no membership on homepage
